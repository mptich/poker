Each game is recorded as 1 line in a CSV file.

Fields:
1. Date
2. Room (PockerStars)
3. Game number
4. Game type (Hold'em No Limit)
5. Currency (USD)
6. SM/BB 
7. Slash separated active players, starting with Small Blind
8. Slash separated amounts in chips for each player
9. Slash separated communal cards (up to 5)
10. Slash separated moves, preflop
11. Flop moves
12. Turn moves
13. River moves
14. Shown cards
15. Pot / rake
16. Players and their gains (losses)
