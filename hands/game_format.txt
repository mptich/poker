Each game is recorded as 1 line in a CSV file.

Fields:
1. Date
2. Room (PockerStars)
3. Game number
4. Game type (Hold'em No Limit)
5. Currency (USD)
6. SM/BB pays
7. Comma separated active players
8. Comma separated amounts in chips for each player
9. Small blind name
10. Hole cards: Comma separated moves 
11. Flop
12. Flop moves
13. Turn
14. Turn moves
15. River
16. River moves
17. Shown cards
18. Pot and rake
19. Players and their gains (losses)
