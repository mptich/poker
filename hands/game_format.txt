Each game is recorded as 1 line in a CSV file.

Fields:
1. Date
2. Room (PockerStars)
3. Game number
4. Game type (Hold'em No Limit)
5. Currency (USD)
6. SM/BB 
7. Ante size
8. Slash separated players, starting with Small Blind
9. Slash separated amounts in chips for each player
10. Additional contributions of non-blinds: <player index>{s|b|sb}
11. Slash separated communal cards (up to 5)
12. Slash separated moves, preflop <player index>{fo|ch|be|ca|ra|un}[amount]
13. Flop moves
14. Turn moves
15. River moves
16. Shown cards <player index>5sTc or mucks <player index>m
17. Pot / rake
18. Players and their gains (losses if negative)
